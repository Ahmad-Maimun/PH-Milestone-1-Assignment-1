Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

digitalization, sustainability, entrepreneurship, automation, e-commerce, education, infrastructure, research, green technologies, and collaboration.

Sustainability

renewable energy, eco-friendly practices, water conservation, recycling, and awareness.

Infrastructure

smart cities, transportation, connectivity, urban development, and sustainable amenities.

Empowerment

education, skills, equality, opportunities, healthcare, and leadership.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Bangladesh's future vision of Green Energy: Using renewable energy, solar, wind, and eco-friendly technologies for sustainability.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Poverty is not created by poor people; it is created by the systems we create. If we want to eradicate poverty, we must change the systems that perpetuate it. We must create opportunities for people to become self-reliant, to unlock their potential, and to break the cycle of poverty. It’s not charity that can bring change, but creating opportunities for people to become entrepreneurs, to empower them to lift themselves. By focusing on social businesses, we can solve problems while generating profits that help improve the lives of the underserved, creating a world of equality and opportunity.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
